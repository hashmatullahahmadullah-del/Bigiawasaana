Afghani & Halal Food Truck — Reseda, Los Angeles CA

Bigi Awasaana is Reseda's premier Afghani and Halal food truck, serving the Los Angeles area including Reseda, Canoga Park, Winnetka, Northridge and the San Fernando Valley. Our menu features authentic Afghani dishes including chicken shawarma, lamb shawarma, mixed kebab platters, seekh kebab, chapli kebab, mantu dumplings, bolani, and fresh tandoor naan.

All our food is 100% Halal certified. We use fresh ingredients daily with no preservatives. Find us in Reseda, CA 91335 and follow us on Instagram for daily location updates.
